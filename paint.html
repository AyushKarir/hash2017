Paint-o-Mania

"Paint the story with colors"

Guidelines


◈ Participant has to create six scenes to depict any fairy tale in TUX PAINT.
◈ Scene number is to be written on each scene created.

Judgment Criteria-
◈ Creativity
◈ Presentation
◈ Color Theme
◈ Use of available tools

Eligibily: IV
1 Team of 2 Students
Teacher Incharge: Ms. Suruchi Batra
