3D-Convenience

"Bringing 3D Models to life with 3D PRINTING"

Online + Onsite


◈This competition is for those who want to show their Design skills in converting an imaginary model into a real-world 3D model.
◈ Design a 3D printable gadget using Sketchup/Tinkercad that will make life easier, simpler, better, more convenient or just more fun.
◈ For Online round, you have to create a .STL file, source file used to actually create that 3D object and a short video of 30 seconds showing participant designing the object.
◈ Upload a zipped folder containing both files on davsbatl@gmail.com latest by April 21, 2018. Mention school name in subject line.
◈ Result of online submission will be uploaded on our event website http://hash.dldavsb.org/ https://www.facebook.com/hashdavsb/ and Facebook page on April 26, 2018.
◈ Selected teams will be invited for Final onsite round.
◈Judgment Criteria-
          → Originality
          → Technique
          → Creativity
          → Innovativeness
